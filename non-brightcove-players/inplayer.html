Introduction

InPlayer is a non-Brightcove player that is optimized for monetization and subscription management.

You can easily deliver your Brightcove videos using InPlayer by following these instructions provided by InPlayer.

Note that Brightcove has no formal relationship with InPlayer and does not support the integration referred to in this article.
